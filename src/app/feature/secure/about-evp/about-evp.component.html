What is Employee Volunteering Portal (EVP) ?

At Asian Paints, employees will contribute their skills, time and efforts, in programmes meant for the betterment of communities around us, including but not restricted to those communities that are also impacted by the organization’s CSR Projects.

Objectives of EVP :

  1. To develop empathy among employees which is a fuel to their passion to do good for others.
  2. In doing so, to encourage them to leverage their skill sets, their time and effort in socio-environmental initiatives meant to engage and impact with communities.
  3. To educate employees about the efforts being taken simultaneously by the organization towards socio-environmental causes.
  4. To work and contribute within the Company’s projects thus creating greater engagement between our employees and the communities we are working with.
  5. To generate a sense of ownership and belongingness for the environment, self-satisfaction through their own efforts & belief in the values of the organization, in its commitment to those around us .

EVP Mission Statement

To influence a culture of Giving, amongst Employees, through Volunteering, for the betterment of communities around us thus bringing Joy and Happiness in their lives
